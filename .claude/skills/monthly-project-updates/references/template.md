## Tips for Good Exec Updates

- Put the "so what" *next to* the data, not in a separate paragraph
- Call out **trajectory changes** (status deltas, new risks, de-scopes)
- Use challenges sparingly: only when they impact milestones or need leadership help
- If a decision is "needs review" or "urgent," include **who** needs to decide and **by when**
